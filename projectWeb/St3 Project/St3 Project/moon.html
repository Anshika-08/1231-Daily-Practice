This Is Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. Together with Earth it forms the Earth-Moon satellite system. In the Solar System it is the fifth largest satellite, larger than any of the known dwarf planets and the largest (and most massive) satellite of a planet relative to the planet. Distance to Earth: 384,400 km Density: 3.34 g/cm³ Radius: 1,737.4 km Age: 4.53 billion years Gravity: 1.62 m/s² Orbits: Earth
